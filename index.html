Executive Summary

My name is Adam Ryder, I am a student at Metropolitan State University of Denver. This portfolio will include details about the work and efforts that I have made throughout the project. The purpose of this portfolio is to illustrate not only what I have contributed to this project, but also what I have learned. Each tab above will direct you to an associated page of "artifacts" and their related annotations. These annotations provide an explanation of what the artifact is and my own personal observations and insights gained from it.


My team's assignment was to produce a piece of software for our client, Comcast. Comcast needed a way to convert all of their existing media files from their current format, MPEG2TS, to a more modern format, MPEG4. So the goal of our project was to produce this software for Comcast. Dr. Neill Kipp acted as the representative of Comcast and was a key stakeholder for this project. We interacted with him throughout the semester to design our software.


Our team's name was Team Runtime, and included the following people: Myself, Chris Buckner, Andrew Canastar, David Firestone, Mathew Guest, and Logan Halverson.
