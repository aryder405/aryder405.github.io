Team Building

These are artifacts that describe our team building activites. These consisted of getting food and drinks at local restaurants. During these social sessions we would try various team building activities.

Title: Benny's Mexican Restaurant
Reflection: This was our first specified team building session. We met here just to socialize and get to know each other better. We ate some good mexican food and had some margaritas. During this session we each talked about some of our strength and weaknesses and also talked about about personal lives. It was nice to get to know my team mates more personally, and it is the basis for establishing trust amongst everyone.


Title: Jack n Grill
Reflection: This was our second specified team building session. This meeting also had an agenda to it to discuss our plans for our first iteration. During this meeting we had a vote about who the team leader should be. This was a specific requirement of our client, because this leader would be his main point of contact for the group. It was a unanimous decision that Andrew would be the leader. I think that if everyone notices the good leadership qualities in a person that everyone unanimously votes for that person to be the leader, then that person should obviously be the leader. It was a good decision, because he was a great leader throughout the project.


Title: Roosevelts
Reflection: After the success of our first presentation, we all decided to go out and celebrate. We picked a bar downtown named Roosevelts. We all had a great time and it really helped gel the team. We didn't explicitly participate in any team building activities, but we did have cause for celebration and this activity alone helps with gelling a team.
